Oh boy, let the text sprawl on and on.

NOTE: You need pygame to run this.

TO RUN: We separated the files into the different stages (eg: main menu, playing, game over)
To open the game, run "python MAIN_MENU.py"
You can set the game resolution or just press enter to default to 1080 x 720
Use the WASD keys to move, aim with the mouse, and click or press the spacebar to fire "bits"


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
INSTRUCTIONS (SINGLE PLAYER):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WASD to move.
Mouse to aim.
Mouse Click to shoot (you can hold).
Shift to sprint.
Press 1 to switch to shield, which can bash enemies
Press 2 to switch to machine gun,
Press 3 to switch to shotgun,
Press Tab to cycle through weapons
Shoot all enemies without getting shot; your lives are in the top right, and the enemy count is at the bottom right.

If you have hardmode on, shoot all the enemies, but keep an eye out on the top left corner...

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
INSTRUCTIONS (TWO PLAYER):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Player 1:
---------
WASD to move.
T and Y to aim.
Automatically shoot (No need to manually do it).

---------
Player 2:
---------
Arrow keys to move.
Right shift and Enter to aim.
Automatically shoot (No need to manually do it).

Shoot the other player. You each have 5 lives. Each is listed in the top corners.
